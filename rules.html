Current Ruleset being used:

  • 1. The object of the game is to score higher than the dealer without exceeding a score of 21

  • 2. The dealer and the player will both be dealt 2 cards initially.

  • 3. The player will be able to see both of their cards but only one of the dealer's cards will be exposed.

  • 4. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are wild initially at a value of 11, but will convert to 1 if the score exceeds 21.

  • 5. When the player chooses to stay, the dealer will take their turn, exposing their cards and drawing until they stay.

  • 6. The dealer will hit on anything less than 17.

  • 7. The deck is an infinate meaning each card has a 1 in 52 chance of coming up. This also allows for the possibility of identical cards (IE. 2 Queen of hearts can be in play).

  • 8. If the score ends with the dealer and the player hands at equal values this is a draw, and there is no winner.

  • 9. At this time Split hands are not implemented.

  • 10. At this time gambling is not implemented.
